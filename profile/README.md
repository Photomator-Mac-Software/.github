## What is Photomator Mac?

Photomator Mac focuses on elegant, intelligent photo enhancement with a strong macOS-native design. It combines **[AI-assisted editing](#)** with clear manual controls for exposure, color and detail adjustments, enabling rapid improvement of everyday photos and professional captures alike. Smart masking, **[subject detection](#)** and object-aware retouching let users apply targeted adjustments without complicated workflows.

The app’s editing engine handles RAW files with respect for dynamic range and **[color accuracy](#)**, making it suitable for high-quality camera workflows. Users can refine tone, correct lens distortion and apply **[advanced color grading](#)** with a smooth real-time preview. Photomator Mac integrates deeply with the Photos library, ensuring seamless import, nondestructive editing and **[sync across devices](#)**.

With quick actions, batch enhancements and **[intelligent presets](#)**, Photomator Mac offers photographers a balanced, modern approach to editing—powerful enough for demanding shots but simple enough for casual adjustments.
